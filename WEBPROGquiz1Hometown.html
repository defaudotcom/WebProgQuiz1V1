Hometown

Jakarta

Jakarta, sometimes known as "J-Town" or "The Big Durian," is Indonesia's largest and most populated metropolis. It is the country's political, economic, and cultural centre and is located on the northwest coast of the island of Java. Jakarta is a dynamic and vibrant city with a rich cultural heritage and a rapidly growing economy. It is a place where traditional Indonesian culture blends with modernity, offering visitors a unique and bustling experience.
